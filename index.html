Betting Arbitrage Project Demo

Scraping and calculating arbitrage opportunities between betting exchanges and major betting companies.

How It Works

The Betting Arbitrage Project Demo offers a comprehensive solution for identifying arbitrage opportunities across major betting companies and sports categories. With support for four major betting companies and coverage of three different sports (football, tennis, basketball), our program scans more than 30 different categories to uncover lucrative opportunities.

We've organized the games into two main categories: Live and Pregame. You can easily navigate between these categories and select your preferred betting exchange to view the available arbitrage opportunities.

Upon accessing the platform, you'll encounter a user-friendly interface displaying the games sorted in descending order based on the arbitrage opportunity. The arbitrage opportunities are highlighted in red, providing clear indicators of potential profits. Additionally, the size of the lay markets is prominently displayed, allowing users to make informed decisions quickly and efficiently.

In the displayed interface, games are organized in a table format with various columns providing detailed information. The columns include:

  • Time: The scheduled start time of the game.
  • Links: Links to the respective betting markets for betting provider and exchange.
  • Game: The teams or players involved in the match.
  • Market: The type of betting market, such as match odds or over/under goals.
  • Selection: The specific bet within the market.
  • Betting Provider odd: The odds offered by specific betting provider
  • Betting Exchange lay odds: The three lays odds from the betting exchange, with the corresponding market sizes displayed underneath.
  • Diff: The difference in odds between betting provider and the best available odds from the betting exchange, indicating the arbitrage opportunity.

The table is designed to highlight the arbitrage opportunities in red, making it easy to spot potential profitable bets. The differences in odds, along with the market sizes, provide a clear view of the value and liquidity available for each arbitrage opportunity.

Technical Information

The Betting Arbitrage Project Demo leverages the power of GO, Python, and Flask, hosted in a robust cloud-based Linux environment. Advanced web scraping techniques, including asynchronous programming and intelligent proxy rotation, ensure efficient and reliable data acquisition from multiple sources simultaneously.

Our system delivers near real-time updates within 1-2 seconds, thanks to the high-performance data processing capabilities of Polars. This enables rapid identification and analysis of arbitrage opportunities, providing users with timely and actionable insights.
